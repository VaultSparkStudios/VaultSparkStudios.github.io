VaultSpark Studios  ·  Careers

Build With Us

VaultSpark Studios is a small, independent studio building games, software, and interactive experiences. We move fast, ship often, and care deeply about quality and craft.

Our Culture

Ship It

We believe in building things and putting them in front of people. Browser-first, free-to-play, no gatekeeping. Every project starts in the Forge and earns its Spark.

Own Your Work

Autonomy is the default. We trust people to make good decisions, manage their own time, and deliver work they are proud of.

Build in the Open

Our code is on GitHub. Our roadmap is public. Our Signal Log documents the journey honestly. Transparency is not a marketing strategy — it is how we operate.

Quality Over Quantity

We would rather ship one polished experience than ten rushed prototypes. Every pixel, every interaction, every line of code matters.

Open Positions

We do not have any open positions at this time. VaultSpark Studios is currently a solo-founder studio with AI-augmented development workflows.

Collaborate With Us

Even when we are not hiring, we are always interested in connecting with talented people. If you are passionate about any of the following, we would love to hear from you:

  • Game Development — browser-based games, JavaScript/TypeScript, canvas/WebGL
  • Frontend Engineering — React, vanilla JS, performance optimization, PWAs
  • Design — UI/UX, visual identity, motion design, game art
  • Music & Audio — game soundtracks, sound effects, ambient audio
  • Writing — game narratives, world-building, lore, technical writing
  • Community — moderation, engagement, content creation

How to Reach Out

Send an email to founder@vaultsparkstudios.com with the subject "Collaboration" and include:

  • A brief introduction — who you are and what you do
  • Links to your work (portfolio, GitHub, Dribbble, etc.)
  • What excites you about VaultSpark Studios
  • What kind of collaboration you are interested in

We read every message and respond to all serious inquiries.

Follow us on GitHub to see what we are building.
