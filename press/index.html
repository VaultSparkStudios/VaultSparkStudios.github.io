Short Bio (150 words)

VaultSpark Studios is an independent game studio building browser-based games, cinematic worlds, and exclusive member experiences. Every project develops under the vault philosophy — sealed in containment, pressurized, and released only when charged enough to ignite. Free to play, instant in the browser, no download required.

The studio launched in March 2026 with Call of Doodie and VaultSpark Football GM, followed by a growing catalog of games in development spanning action comedy, sports simulation, strategy, roguelite RPG, survival narrative, and puzzle genres. A cross-game member system called the Vault tracks player progress across every title with a 9-tier rank system.

Community Platform

The Vault Member System

VaultSpark Studios operates a cross-game membership platform called the Vault. A free account follows players across every studio title — tracking Vault Rank, achievements, challenge completions, and early access drops. The 9-tier rank system runs from Spark Initiate to The Sparked (100,000+ points), rewarding long-term engagement with increasingly exclusive access.

Optional paid tiers (VaultSparked from $4.99/mo; VaultSparked Eternal from $29.99/mo) add premium benefits including exclusive lore drops, Discord role sync, and Phase 1 grandfather pricing locked for early members. The Vault is the connective tissue binding every VaultSpark title into a single, expanding universe.
