Studio Boilerplate

VaultSpark Studios is an independent game studio forging cinematic worlds, sports simulations, and playable browser experiments. Founded in 2026, VaultSpark builds free-to-play browser games powered by a proprietary Studio OS designed to ship at team speed from a lean foundation. Every project begins sealed in the vault and is sparked into existence — from action comedy chaos to deep sports franchise simulations and a fantasy MMORPG rising from the dark. The vault is sparked.

Games

Call of Doodie — Sparked Action comedy browser shooter. A parody-fueled arcade experience built for laughs and surprisingly sharp gameplay. Free to play instantly, no download required.
Gridiron GM — Vaulted Football franchise management simulation. Build a dynasty across multiple seasons — draft, trade, sign, and cut players. Every decision has consequences.
VaultSpark Football GM — Sparked Deep NFL front-office simulation focused on league realism, career arcs, depth charts, and franchise storytelling.
VaultFront — In The Forge Strategic warfront with tactical territory control and resource management. Backend in development.
Solara — In The Forge Fantasy MMORPG set in a realm of shadowed horizons and strange beauty. Active world-building phase.
