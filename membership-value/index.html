What's next

Proposed pricing innovations

The current structure is strong but has gaps. Here are the most compelling improvements — ranked by impact and feasibility.

High impact
Annual Plans
$44.99/yr Sparked · $269.99/yr Eternal
Offer annual billing at a 25% discount. Reduces churn, improves cash flow predictability, and appeals to committed members who don't want monthly billing anxiety.
No architecture changes needed — Stripe supports annual price IDs. Two new price IDs + UI toggle.
Bold move
Vault Lifetime Pass
$299 one-time · All Sparked perks, forever
One-time payment for permanent Sparked access with a unique "Vault Lifer" badge and title. Zero churn risk. Superfan signal. Equivalent to ~5 years of $4.99/mo — great deal for the member, great LTV for the studio.
Reduces ongoing revenue per seat but captures superfans who would otherwise churn. Separate from grandfather pricing (no monthly billing, no rate lock needed).
Fills the gap
Mid-Tier: Vault Signal
$9.99/mo — between Sparked and Eternal
The $4.99 → $29.99 jump is 6×. A $9.99 mid-tier bridges the gap for members who want more than Sparked but aren't ready for $30/mo. Includes: Sparked perks + monthly dev log video + feature voting + 750 XP bonus.
Captures the "I'd pay $10" segment that currently converts to Free. Minimal unique content — existing assets (dev logs, voting) structured into a tier.
Quick win
Rank Loyalty Discount
Forge Master: −20% off Sparked · The Sparked: −35%
Members who reach high ranks through engagement earn a discount on their first Sparked subscription. Creates a powerful conversion funnel: engage → rank up → unlock cheaper access → subscribe.
Rewards the most active free members. Makes the rank system directly affect real money — massive incentive to stay engaged. Discount = Stripe coupon applied at checkout.
Community
Gift Packs (Expanded)
3-mo Sparked: $12.99 · 1-yr Sparked: $44.99
Gift checkout exists but only offers monthly cadence. Adding 3-month and annual gift options unlocks birthday/holiday gifting behaviour. Gaming gifts are a proven market — especially for friends who won't subscribe themselves.
Gift subs are viral — the recipient becomes a new community member. Stripe supports gift receipts and delayed subscription starts.
Long-term
Studio Supporter Bundle
$79/yr — one-time annual contribution
Non-recurring annual "Studio Support" payment. Unlocks: VaultSparked for 1 year + a permanent "Studio Supporter" badge. No recurring billing, no grandfather lock, no Eternal perks. Appeals to people allergic to subscriptions.
Removes subscription friction for a segment of the market. Effectively same as annual Sparked but framed as a "support" action — psychologically different from a "subscription".
◆
Immediate priority: annual plans.

Annual billing is the single highest-impact change. It reduces churn by 40–60% industry-wide, increases LTV, and the 25% discount pays for itself in retention. Two Stripe price IDs + a UI toggle = done.
