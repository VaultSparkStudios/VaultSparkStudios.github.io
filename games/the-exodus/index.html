The Exodus

Get Early Signal All Games

Lead Them Through The Fracture.

The Exodus is a narrative survival experience where you guide a displaced people through a collapsing world. Resources are scarce. Trust is fragile. Every decision you make determines who survives, who you lose, and what kind of society rises from the ashes.

This isn't a game about winning. It's a game about what you're willing to sacrifice to keep your people alive — and whether the world you build afterward was worth the cost.

  • Dynamic faction system — every survivor has beliefs, skills, and breaking points
  • Procedurally generated crisis events that force impossible moral decisions
  • Resource management with cascading consequences across generations
  • Branching narrative paths that permanently alter the world state
  • No save-scumming — every playthrough is a permanent record of your leadership
  • Vault Members unlock exclusive lore fragments and early chapter access
