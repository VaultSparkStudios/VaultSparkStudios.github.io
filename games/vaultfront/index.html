VaultFront

View Project Get Early Access

Control the Vault. Break the Front.

VaultFront is a real-time strategy warfront built around vault capture mechanics and convoy timing. Asymmetric design means controlling a single chokepoint can flip the entire battle state. Every positioning decision carries swing potential — the pressure builds until something breaks.

  • Real-time vault capture — control strategic chokepoints to reverse battle momentum
  • Convoy timing mechanics — when you move matters as much as where you move
  • Asymmetric strategy — one chokepoint can decide the whole fight
  • Browser-native — no download, built for the web from the ground up
  • Backend runtime in active development on VaultSpark studio infrastructure
  • Early access available through Vault Membership
