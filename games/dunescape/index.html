Every Horizon Is A Door.

Dunescape is VaultSpark's most ambitious title — a persistent fantasy MMORPG set in a realm of shadowed horizons and ancient myth. An open world where exploration and character progression define every session. Not a room. Not a level. A world. One where the next horizon is always worth walking toward.

  • Exploration-first design — every horizon conceals something worth finding
  • Deep character progression — your arc is yours to define
