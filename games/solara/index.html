Solara: Sunfall

Join the Waitlist All Games

Every Death Dims The Sun.

Solara: Sunfall is a browser roguelite RPG where every player death dims a shared sun. The world darkens collectively — when enough players fall, the light fades for everyone. Run the daily dungeon, leave your grave on the living map, and fight to keep the light alive. No download required — the sun loads in your browser.

The world of Solara has no reset button. Every grave you leave behind becomes a permanent marker on the living map. Other players will find your bones, loot your remains, and wonder what killed you. The sun dims with each death — and only victories push the light back.

  • Browser roguelite RPG with a shared sun that dims with every player death across the server
  • Daily dungeon runs — procedurally generated, one shot per day, global leaderboard
  • Living graveyard map — your death leaves a permanent grave other players can discover
  • Fight to keep the light alive — collective victories push the sun back from the edge
  • Permadeath with legacy rewards — each run makes your next character stronger
  • Vault Points integration — your VaultSpark rank carries cross-game prestige into Solara

Release Window: Target: Late 2026 early access — Vault Members get first invites.
