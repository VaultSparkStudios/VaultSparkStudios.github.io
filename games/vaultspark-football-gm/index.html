The League Never Sleeps.

VaultSpark Football GM is a deep persistent NFL-style franchise management sim. Unlike session-based sims, your league is saved to the browser — come back tomorrow, next week, whenever. Run multiple leagues simultaneously with multiple save slots. Choose between Drive Sim (possession-by-possession) or Play Sim (individual play resolution) for exactly the level of detail you want.

Apply Quick Start presets — Modern, Balanced, or Legacy — to get your league running in seconds, or configure every detail from scratch. When game day arrives, the field resolves itself. You're the GM making the calls that shape the roster, the scheme, and the legacy.

  • Persistent leagues — save your franchise and return across browser sessions
  • Multiple save slots — run and manage multiple leagues simultaneously
  • Drive Sim vs Play Sim — choose possession-by-possession or individual play resolution
  • Quick Start presets — Modern, Balanced, and Legacy league configurations
  • League Hub — centralized menu to start, resume, or continue your active franchise
  • Multi-season dynasty — players age, contract values shift, career arcs develop
  • Server-backed mode available when VaultSpark backend infrastructure goes live
  • Free in your browser — no download, no account required to start
