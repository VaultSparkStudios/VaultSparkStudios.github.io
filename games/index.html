Frequently Asked Questions

Are VaultSpark games free to play?

Yes. All live VaultSpark games are completely free to play in your web browser. No download, no installation, no paywall. A free Studio Member account unlocks additional features and cross-game tracking.

What kinds of games does VaultSpark make?

VaultSpark Studios makes browser-based games across multiple genres: action comedy (Call of Doodie), football franchise sim (Gridiron GM), NFL front-office sim (VaultSpark Football GM), real-time strategy (VaultFront), and fantasy MMORPG (Dunescape). Every game is cinematic, immersive, and built with one spark.

What's the difference between Gridiron GM and VaultSpark Football GM?

Gridiron GM is a session-based front-office management sim set in a fictional NFL-style universe — pick a team, draft players, manage the cap, and watch your season simulate with animated play-by-play. There's no on-field gameplay; you're the GM calling shots from the front office. VaultSpark Football GM is a persistent simulation with saved leagues, multi-season career arcs, and a full franchise hub you can return to across sessions.

Can I play VaultSpark games on my phone?

VaultSpark games are web-first experiences that run in modern browsers. While most games work on mobile devices, the best experience is on desktop. Mobile optimization varies by title — check each game's page for details.

How do I get early access to games in development?

Create a free Vault Member account to get early access drops, beta invites, and first signals when sealed projects open. Studio Members are the first to know when something new sparks out of the vault.

Will VaultSpark games ever come to Steam or mobile app stores?

VaultSpark's current focus is on web-first browser experiences. Console and mobile app distribution may come for select titles as the studio grows. Follow the studio's channels for announcements.
