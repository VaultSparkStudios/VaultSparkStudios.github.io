MindFrame

Get Early Signal All Games

Every Level Is A Question.

MindFrame is a first-of-its-kind cognitive puzzle game where the rules of each level aren't explained — they're discovered. Built around the mechanics of perceptual inference, every chamber presents you with a world that follows consistent but hidden logic. Your job isn't to beat the level. It's to understand it.

What sets MindFrame apart is its adaptive difficulty engine: the game observes how you approach problems and recalibrates — not to make it easier, but to keep you in the exact zone where understanding feels possible but not guaranteed.

  • 60+ chambers across 5 perceptual domains: spatial, temporal, social, symbolic, and sensory
  • No tutorial, no instruction text — discovery is the only path forward
  • Adaptive inference engine that learns your individual problem-solving patterns
  • Competitive mode: race other players to crack the same hidden ruleset first
  • Vault Members who complete a chapter unlock a unique "MindFrame Initiate" achievement
  • Developer commentary mode — post-completion, the designer explains every chamber's logic

Release Window: Target: Mid 2027 — concept phase with internal prototype underway.
