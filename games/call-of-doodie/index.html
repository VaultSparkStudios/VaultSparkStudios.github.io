Chaos Is The Mechanic.

Call of Doodie is a hilarious browser shooter RPG where absurdity is the primary mechanic and punchlines arrive via ammunition. A parody-fueled arcade experience built for laughs, chaos, and surprisingly sharp gameplay. Enter a world where the comedy never stops and the action never lets up.
