The Front Office. The Draft. The Dynasty.

Gridiron GM is a deep NFL-style franchise management sim built around the systems that make real front offices tick. Run a 7-round draft using the Jimmy Johnson pick value chart. Hire scouts with nine different specialties and see three draft classes ahead. Set coaching schemes — Air Raid, Power Run, West Coast, Zone Blitz — and watch scheme fit shape your sim outcomes. Manage a $300M salary cap across a 42-man roster. Every season ages your players, shrinks contracts, and forces hard roster decisions.

When game day arrives, resolve instantly or watch the action unfold in ⚡ Live Sim — an animated, broadcast-style simulation with a visual SVG field, drive tracking, and a Player of the Game reveal. You call the shots from the front office; the field runs itself. 32 fictional teams. 10 positions. Fully procedurally generated rosters where every franchise is unique.

  • 7-round NFL draft with Jimmy Johnson pick value chart governing all trades
  • Scouting department — 9 specialist types, 3-year draft class lookahead
  • Coaching schemes with real scheme-fit bonuses affecting sim outcomes
  • $300M salary cap with dead cap penalties, contracts, and FA negotiations
  • ⚡ Live Sim — watch games simulate with animated play-by-play on a visual SVG field
  • Multi-season dynasty — players age, retire, and stats accumulate
  • 32 fictional teams, procedurally generated players, real passer rating formula
  • Returning soon — Vault Members get first access when the doors reopen
