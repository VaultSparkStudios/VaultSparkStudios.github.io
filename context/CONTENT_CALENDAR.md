## Post Template

Each journal post follows this structure:
1. **Hero image** — branded OG image (1200x630)
2. **Opening hook** — 1-2 sentences that draw the reader in
3. **Body** — 400-800 words, broken into 2-3 sections with subheadings
4. **Call to action** — join the vault, play a game, follow on social
5. **Meta** — JSON-LD Article schema, OG tags, canonical URL
