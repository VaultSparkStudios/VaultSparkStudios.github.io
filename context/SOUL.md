# Soul

## Core promise

The Vault is a place you earn your way into. Every action — visiting a game, reading lore, referring a friend — deepens your membership and reveals more of the world. Being VaultSparked means you're not just a fan; you're a founding signal in something being built.

## Non-negotiables

- must always be true: the portal feels exclusive, atmospheric, and worth being in — not corporate or generic
- audience should feel: like they discovered something, like their membership has weight, like the rank they hold was earned
- quality bar: dark, precise UI with consistent Vault/Portal naming. No cluttered layouts. Every feature should feel intentional.

## Anti-goals

- must never drift into: a generic gaming forum or points-grind app with no identity
- cheap imitation to avoid: do not make it look or feel like a Discord server clone or a Patreon page
- tonal failure mode: over-explaining the lore or rank system — the mystery is part of the value
