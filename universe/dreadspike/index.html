DreadSpike

The being called from the dark by a wrong transmission. He was not summoned. He arrived.

Watch Cinematic on Sora Get Lore Dispatches

DreadSpike Cinematic — Sora-generated, 2026 — VaultSpark Studios Original

Origin

Crimson light cuts across frozen space. Fiery ash drifts in suspension. Every movement feels ritualistic — not violence, but ceremony. Every strike feels fated, not chosen. When DreadSpike enters the void, the stars don't just go dark. They pull back.

DreadSpike was not summoned. He was called — accidentally, catastrophically, and irreversibly — by a wrong transmission sent across a dimensional threshold that should have remained closed. The signal reached something that should never have been reached. What answered was not what was expected.

He arrived in a realm unprepared for him — a dimension that issued a signal meant for something else entirely, and received a being of mythic, terrifying precision. The vault cannot undo the call. It can only contain what arrived. Now the question isn't whether he can be sent back. The question is what he intends to do while he's here.
