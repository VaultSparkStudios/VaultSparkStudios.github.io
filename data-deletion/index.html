VaultSpark Studios  ·  Your Data

Data Deletion Request

Under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR, CCPA, and others), you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. Use the form below or email us directly.

What Gets Deleted

When we process a deletion request, we remove:

  • Account data — your Vault Member profile, username, email, preferences, and settings
  • Points & rank history — all Vault Points, rank progression, and leaderboard entries
  • Newsletter subscription — your email is removed from Vault Dispatch and all mailing lists
  • Contact messages — any messages sent through our contact form

What We May Retain

We may retain limited data where required by law, for fraud prevention, or to enforce our Terms of Service:

  • Records needed for legal compliance or active disputes
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data (not linked to your identity)
  • Backup copies that are automatically purged within 90 days

Submit a Request

Your deletion request has been submitted. We will process it within 30 days and confirm via email.

We will send a verification email before processing your request.

Alternative Methods

You can also submit a deletion request by:

  • Email: Send to founder@vaultsparkstudios.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
  • Contact form: Use our contact page and select "Data Request" as the topic

Timeline

We process all data deletion requests within 30 days of verification. You will receive email confirmation when your data has been deleted. If we need additional information to verify your identity, the timeline begins after verification is complete.

For questions about data rights, see our Privacy Policy or contact founder@vaultsparkstudios.com.
