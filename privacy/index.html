Privacy Policy & Legal Notices

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This page covers how VaultSpark Studios collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you visit vaultsparkstudios.com or interact with a VaultSpark service. It also explains our copyright, trademark, fan-content, and reporting notices so there is no implied license to reuse VaultSpark material without permission.

Privacy Policy

Who We Are

VaultSpark Studios is an independent game studio operating at vaultsparkstudios.com. All inquiries regarding this policy may be directed to founder@vaultsparkstudios.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Email address and contact details — when you subscribe to Vault Dispatch, register a Vault Member account, request support, or contact the studio.
  • Vault Member account data — username, member number, profile details, newsletter preferences, rank/points-related activity, and account settings stored in our managed backend.
  • Device-stored preferences — UI settings such as your chosen site theme, card theme, consent state, and session helpers stored in browser storage so the site remembers your setup on that device.
  • Usage and diagnostic data — page views, session flow, browser/device information, approximate location, referrer data, and basic operational diagnostics collected through analytics and platform logs.
  • Security and anti-abuse signals — login/session metadata, rate-limit or abuse-prevention events, and challenge/verification status used to protect accounts and site integrity.
  • Contact messages — if you use the contact flow, your message contents and reply details are used solely to respond and maintain the conversation record.

Vault Member Accounts & Preferences

Vault Member accounts are handled through our authentication and database stack. When you sign up, sign in, or use account features, your account record is stored in our backend and certain convenience data is also stored locally in your browser to keep you signed in and preserve personal settings.

If you choose a site theme while signed into a Vault Member account, that preference may be stored both on the device you are using and in your account preferences so it can follow you to other devices when appropriate.

Newsletter & Email Providers

Vault Dispatch and member email flows may be processed through third-party email service providers used by VaultSpark Studios, including Kit and operational delivery vendors such as Resend. Those providers act only as delivery infrastructure for studio communications.

We use your email to send:

  • Studio updates, game launches, and announcements
  • Lore dispatches and universe reveals
  • Early access drops for Vault Members

You may unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of any email, or by emailing founder@vaultsparkstudios.com with "Unsubscribe" in the subject line.

Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect anonymous, aggregated data. This data includes:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Browser type, device category, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic region (country/city level)
  • Referral source (how you arrived at the site)

This data is processed by Google under their Privacy Policy. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings.

Cookies

VaultSpark Studios uses the following cookies:

  • Google Analytics cookies (e.g., _ga, _ga_*) — used for analytics as described above. These are first-party cookies set by Google Analytics scripts on our domain.
  • Browser storage — localStorage and related browser storage are used for session continuity, theme preferences such as vs_theme, consent state, and other UX settings. Some of those settings may also be synced to your account if you are signed in.
  • Security or verification tokens — challenge and session-related storage may be used to help prevent abuse, spam, or unauthorized access.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels from third-party ad networks, or behavioural targeting technology.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send newsletters and updates you opted into
  • To create and operate Vault Member accounts, rankings, and profile settings
  • To remember your chosen experience, including theme and account preferences
  • To respond to contact form submissions and inquiries
  • To improve the site through aggregate analytics, testing, and product decisions
  • To detect abuse, protect accounts, and maintain platform reliability

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Service Providers

We rely on third-party infrastructure providers to run parts of the Service, including hosting, analytics, authentication/database services, email delivery, and payment or anti-abuse tooling where relevant. These providers may process data on our behalf only to deliver the services we use.

Data Retention

Email subscriber data is retained while you remain subscribed or until deletion is requested. Vault Member account data is retained for as long as your account remains active and for a reasonable period afterward for security, backup, legal, or abuse-prevention needs. Browser-stored preferences remain on your device until you clear them or they are overwritten. Contact messages are retained as long as needed to respond and maintain correspondence records. Analytics data is retained according to the settings of the tools we use.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we process, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own devices, browser, and account credentials.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to withdraw consent (e.g., unsubscribe from emails)
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (EU/EEA residents)

To exercise any of these rights, contact founder@vaultsparkstudios.com.

Children's Privacy

VaultSpark Studios does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Third-Party Links

The VaultSpark Studios website contains links to external platforms (GitHub, YouTube, Discord, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms. Please review their respective privacy policies.

International Use

VaultSpark Studios operates from the United States. If you access the Service from another region, you understand that your information may be processed in jurisdictions that may not provide the same privacy protections as your home jurisdiction.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
