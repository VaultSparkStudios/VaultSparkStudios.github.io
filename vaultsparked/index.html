Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to be a Vault member first?
Yes. VaultSparked upgrades your existing free account. Create your free account first, then upgrade from the portal settings.
What happens when I cancel?
You drop back to the free tier. Your rank, points, and earned achievements stay — you just lose the Sparked perks going forward.
When does the monthly bonus XP drop?
On the first day of each calendar month, 500 points are credited to your vault balance automatically. No action required.
Do Sparked achievements stay on my profile if I cancel?
Yes — any achievement you earned during an active Sparked subscription is permanently on your profile. Achievements are never removed.
Is there a free trial?
There's no formal trial period, but the free tier is genuinely full-featured. Try the Vault free and upgrade when you're ready.
Is billing secure?
Payments are processed by Stripe. VaultSpark Studios never stores your card details. Cancel or update billing any time from your portal.
