Questions

Frequently asked

What is grandfather pricing?
When you subscribe during Phase 1, your price is locked permanently. When Phase 2 opens at a higher price, only new subscribers pay more. You pay $4.99/mo (Sparked) or $29.99/mo (Pro) forever, as long as you keep your subscription active.
What happens if I cancel and resubscribe?
If you cancel and resubscribe later, you'll be placed in the current active phase at the current price. Your grandfather rate only holds while the subscription remains continuously active.
Do I need a free account first?
Yes. VaultSparked and Pro upgrade your existing free Vault account. Create your free account first, then upgrade from your portal settings.
What happens when I cancel?
You drop back to the free tier at the end of your billing period. Your rank, points, and earned achievements stay permanently — you just lose the Sparked/Eternal perks going forward. Your name in the Vault contributor record also remains.
How many Phase 1 spots are there?
Phase 1 VaultSparked is capped at 500 subscribers. Phase 1 VaultSparked Eternal is capped at 100. When a cap is hit, Phase 2 opens at the higher price. Existing members keep their rate forever.
When does the monthly bonus XP drop?
Points are credited automatically on each subscription renewal cycle — 500 pts for Sparked, 1,000 pts for Eternal. No action required.
What is the Genesis Vault Member badge?
The Genesis Vault Member badge is a permanent, non-purchasable achievement awarded to the first 100 public members who joined VaultSpark Studios. It grants +500 bonus XP and is stamped permanently on your vault profile — it cannot be earned after the first 100 spots are filled.
Does VaultSparked Eternal include everything in VaultSparked?
Yes — Eternal is a superset. You get all Sparked perks plus the Eternal-exclusive features (cross-product access, Eternal beta builds, permanent studio credits, and the Eternal Discord role).
Is billing secure?
Payments are processed by Stripe. VaultSpark Studios never stores your card details. Cancel or update billing any time from your portal settings.
