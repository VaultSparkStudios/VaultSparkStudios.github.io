The Vault Ranks

Earn Vault Points and climb through the ranks. Every tier unlocks new perks and recognition.

⚡
Spark Initiate
0 – 249 Vault Points
Full access to the Vault Member dashboard, participate in challenges, and start earning points. The vault is open.
🏃
Vault Runner
250 – 999 Vault Points
Community recognition badge and exclusive Vault Runner Discord role. The vault takes notice.
📏
Rift Scout
1,000 – 2,999 Vault Points
Rift Scout Discord role, +10% point bonus on all completed challenges. You're scouting deeper.
🛡️
Vault Guard
3,000 – 7,499 Vault Points
Vault Guard Discord role and early access to development dispatches. Defending the vault's signal.
🔧
Vault Breacher
7,500 – 14,999 Vault Points
Beta test priority and exclusive Vault Breacher Discord role. You go where others can't.
🕵️
Void Operative
15,000 – 29,999 Vault Points
Void Operative Discord role and access to sealed project previews. Clearance level: deep.
🔐
Vault Keeper
30,000 – 59,999 Vault Points
VIP Discord channel, ability to suggest new community challenges, and a glowing elite profile border.
🔥
Forge Master
60,000 – 99,999 Vault Points
Forge Master Discord role, first access to lore reveals, and direct studio channel. The vault forged you.
✨
The Sparked
100,000+ Vault Points
Gold crown on the leaderboard, permanent Hall of Fame entry, exclusive lore, and direct studio contact. Maximum rank — achieved.

Vault Points  ·  Ways to Earn

How to Earn Points

Points accumulate across everything you do in the vault. Here are the main ways to earn.

📅
Daily Login Bonus
10 pts per day
🔥
Login Streak Milestones
7-day: 50 · 14-day: 100 · 30-day: 200 · 60-day: 500 · 100-day: 1,000
🏁
Complete Challenges
20 pts per challenge + streak bonuses
📖
Read Lore Files
20 pts per file
🎮
Play Games & Submit Scores
XP awarded per session + score submissions
👥
Refer New Members
Milestone rewards at 1, 3, 5, 10, 25 referrals
🎉
Anniversary Bonus
Bonus XP every year in the vault
🎨
Fan Art & Community
Submit fan art, vote, join co-op teams
✨
VaultSparked Subscription
+50% multiplier on all points
