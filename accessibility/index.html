VaultSpark Studios  ·  Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: April 3, 2026

VaultSpark Studios is committed to making vaultsparkstudios.com accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology. We believe the vault should be open to everyone.

Our Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standard. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more usable for all users.

What We Do

  • Semantic HTML — we use proper heading hierarchy, landmarks, and ARIA attributes throughout the site
  • Keyboard navigation — all interactive elements (navigation, buttons, links, forms) are reachable and operable via keyboard
  • Skip links — a "Skip to main content" link is available on every page to bypass repeated navigation
  • Alt text — all meaningful images include descriptive alternative text
  • Color contrast — text and interactive elements meet or exceed WCAG AA contrast ratios
  • Responsive design — the site adapts to screen sizes from mobile to ultrawide, with no horizontal scroll at standard zoom levels
  • Reduced motion — animations respect the prefers-reduced-motion media query where applicable
  • Focus indicators — visible focus outlines are present on all interactive elements
  • Form labels — all form fields include associated labels or aria-label attributes

Known Limitations

While we strive for full compliance, some areas may not yet fully meet WCAG 2.1 AA:

  • Browser-based games — interactive canvas-based games (Call of Doodie, VaultSpark Football GM) may not be fully accessible to screen readers due to the nature of real-time game rendering
  • Third-party embeds — content embedded from external services (YouTube, Discord widgets) is subject to those platforms' accessibility practices
  • Legacy pages — some older journal entries or archived content may have inconsistent heading structure

We actively work to identify and resolve these issues as part of our ongoing development process.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

This site has been designed to be compatible with:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Browser text resizing up to 200%

Feedback & Contact

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, we want to hear about it. Please contact us so we can address it:

  • Email: founder@vaultsparkstudios.com (subject: "Accessibility")
  • Contact form: vaultsparkstudios.com/contact

When reporting an issue, please include the page URL, what you were trying to do, and the assistive technology you were using. We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.

Enforcement

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may wish to contact your local equality or civil rights enforcement body, or file a complaint with the appropriate regulatory agency in your jurisdiction.

Questions? Contact founder@vaultsparkstudios.com.
